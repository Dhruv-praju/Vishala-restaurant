Relish the Best Indian Thalis!

Embark on a culinary journey with our authentic Indian Vegetarian Thalis, crafted to perfection with a harmonious blend of flavors and tradition. Each thali is thoughtfully prepared with fresh, high-quality ingredients, offering a wholesome and satisfying dining experience. We proudly present two signature options to delight your taste buds:
Vishala Thali ($11.99): A generous spread featuring four different curries, lentils, rice, roti, crispy papad, sweet or salted lassi, and a special sweet dish.
Kathiawadi Thali ($12.99): A rustic and flavorful offering that captures the essence of Gujarat with hearty curries, lentils, bajra rotla, garlic chutney, ghee-laden rice, papad, and a traditional sweet dish.

Our Signature Delights

Starters to Savor: Crispy samosas, dahi puri, and freshly grilled paneer tikka.
Curries to Crave: Butter paneer, aloo sabzi, and authentic Gujarati kadhi.
Breads and Rice: Soft, flaky parathas, bajra rotla, and fragrant pulao.
Desserts to Delight: Sweet treats like gulab jamun, shrikhand, and basundi.
Beverages to Refresh: Masala chai, sweet lassi, and fresh sugarcane juice.
